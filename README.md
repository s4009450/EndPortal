## What is a Minecraft End Portal?

The **End Portal** is a structure in Minecraft that teleports players to **The End** — a dark, outer dimension and home of the final boss, the Ender Dragon. It consists of 12 frame blocks arranged in a square, each requiring an Eye of Ender to activate. Once all 12 eyes are placed, the portal opens, revealing a swirling void of dark green and black that players can jump through to begin the final challenge of the game.
